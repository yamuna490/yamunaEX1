THE HUNGER CRISIS

The Feeding India Hunger Report provides a sobering snapshot of food insecurity in India. Released annually during anti-poverty week in October, the Feeding India Hunger Report looks at the prevalence of food poverty around our country. These are the facts about hunger in our country…

194 million Indians are undernourished. Recently out of 117 countries, India ranked at 104 at the Global Hunger Index.

WHAT WE DO?

Feeding India is India’s largest food relief organisation, operating on a scale that makes it crucial to the work of the front line charities who are feeding vulnerable Indians. Feeding India provides more than 70% of the food rescued for food relief organisations nation-wide.
